The Case in Brief 摘要

Fuggler's apparel category on Amazon has underperformed relative to its plush range, and we believe the primary cause is a lack of dedicated social media visibility for the fashion vertical. Fuggler currently has no standalone social channel owned by Fir Ventures that speaks to fashion-conscious consumers. All existing social media activity runs through ZURU's channels, which focus on plush toys and require a 10–14 business-day approval window for any new posts — a timeline fundamentally at odds with fast-fashion marketing rhythms.

Fuggler服飾品類在Amazon上的表現遠遜於毛絨品類，我們認為主要原因在於時裝垂類缺乏獨立的社交媒體曝光。目前Fir Ventures並無獨立管理的社媒賬號用於觸達時尚消費者。所有社媒活動均通過ZURU的賬號發佈，且須經10至14個工作日審批，與快時尚的行銷節奏格格不入。

This plan proposes that Libertas grants Fir Ventures permission to create and operate dedicated Fuggler fashion accounts on Instagram and TikTok, targeting North America, Europe, and the Middle East. The strategic logic is grounded in what POP MART — the world's leading IP-driven toy brand — has proved at scale: localised, category-specific social accounts are a prerequisite for conquering new consumer segments.

本計劃建議Libertas授權Fir Ventures開設並運營Fuggler時裝垂類的Instagram及TikTok獨立賬號，主攻北美、歐洲及中東市場。此戰略邏輯有全球最成功的IP玩具品牌——泡泡瑪特為參照：本地化、品類化的社媒矩陣，正是其征服新消費群體的核心路徑。

01 · The Problem

Why Fuggler Apparel Needs Its Own Voice 為何服飾品類需要獨立社媒賬號

There are three structural reasons the apparel category is not performing on Amazon, and they are all upstream of any product-quality question.

服飾品類在Amazon表現欠佳，有三個結構性原因，且均在產品品質問題之前。

No Fashion-Native Content

ZURU's accounts are plush-toy accounts. They speak to collectors. Apparel buyers are a different consumer — they discover via outfit posts, styling reels, and creator hauls. That content doesn't exist for Fuggler apparel.

ZURU的賬號定位為毛絨玩具賬號，服飾消費者通過穿搭帖、Reels及達人開箱發現品牌，這類內容在Fuggler服飾垂類幾乎為零。

10–14 Day Approval Lag

Fast fashion lives and dies on trend speed. A 10–14 business-day review cycle makes it impossible to react to viral moments, seasonal windows, or influencer opportunities. By the time content is approved, the moment has passed.

快時尚依賴速度。10至14個工作日的審批週期令我們無法及時跟進熱點、把握季節節點或KOL合作機會。

No Direct KOL Pipeline

Without a brand-owned account, approaching fashion creators ("can you tag a brand with no IG?") is nearly impossible. Creators need a verified page to tag, a bio link to drive traffic, and evidence that the brand is active online.

沒有品牌賬號，與時尚KOL建聯幾乎不可能。達人需要可標記的賬號、可跳轉的Bio鏈接，以及品牌活躍的線上存在感。

02 · Market Proof — POP MART Case Study

The POP MART Playbook: From Plush to Global Fashion Brand 泡泡瑪特案例：從毛絨玩具到全球時尚品牌

POP MART is the most instructive comparable in the IP-collectibles-to-lifestyle space. Its annual reports from 2020 to 2025 document a clear, data-backed trajectory: social-first expansion, especially on TikTok, directly drove overseas revenue from near zero to RMB 16.3 billion in five years. The mechanism is directly applicable to Fuggler.

泡泡瑪特是IP潮玩跨界生活方式賽道中最具參考價值的案例。其2020至2025年財報清晰記錄了一條有數據支撐的軌跡：以社媒為核心的海外擴張——尤其是TikTok——直接推動海外收入在五年內從接近零增長至163億人民幣。這一路徑對Fuggler具有直接的借鑑意義。

The MENA account (@popmartmena) is particularly instructive: POP MART launched it with only 10K followers and 420 posts — proof that you start local accounts to build a market, you don't wait until the market is built. This is exactly what Fir Ventures is proposing for Fuggler's fashion presence.

MENA賬號（@popmartmena）尤具參考價值：泡泡瑪特在僅有1萬粉絲、420篇貼文時便已開設此賬號——這證明本地賬號的邏輯是「先建賬號再拓市場」，而非「等市場成熟再開賬號」。這正是Fir Ventures為Fuggler時裝賬號所提出的邏輯。

⚠ A Candid Note on Our Starting Position / 坦誠說明我們的起點

We acknowledge that POP MART is a vertically-integrated brand with full IP ownership and substantial marketing budgets. Fir Ventures is a licensee operating in a more constrained structure. However, the principle — that localised, category-specific accounts outperform a single global channel — is platform-agnostic and has been validated by every major consumer brand that has successfully globalised on social media. We are not claiming to replicate POP MART's scale; we are borrowing its structural logic.

我們承認泡泡瑪特是擁有完整IP版權和充裕預算的垂直整合品牌，而Fir Ventures作為被授權方，運營結構更受約束。然而，「本地化、品類化賬號優於單一全球賬號」這一原則不依賴於預算規模，而是每一個成功全球化的消費品牌都已驗證的社媒運營邏輯。我們借鑑的是其結構邏輯，而非其體量。

04 · The Proposal

What We Are Asking For, and What We Will Do With It 我們的申請內容與執行方案

Fir Ventures requests permission from Libertas to create and independently operate two social media accounts focused on Fuggler's fashion and apparel category:

Fir Ventures請求Libertas授權，創建並獨立運營兩個專注於Fuggler服飾品類的社媒賬號：

Platform Account Focus Primary Region Content Type
Instagram Fuggler Fashion / Apparel North America, Europe, Middle East Outfit posts, 3D design previews, KOL reposts, Reels
TikTok Fuggler Fashion / Apparel North America, Europe, Middle East Styling videos, trend reactions, KOL collabs, SHEIN hauls

Key Operational Differentiators 核心運營優勢

🚀 Trend-Speed Posting

No approval lag. We post in real-time response to trends, seasonal moments, and viral opportunities. Fashion moves fast; our accounts will too.

無需審批等待，實時跟進熱點、季節節點及爆款機會。

🎯 Social Sampling ("社媒測款")

We publish 3D design renders on social before production. Likes and comments serve as real-time demand signals. High-engagement designs go direct to SHEIN's supply chain for small-batch OEM orders.

先在社媒發佈3D設計圖，以互動數據作為生產決策依據，高熱設計直接通過SHEIN供應鏈小批量追單。

🤝 Direct KOL Outreach

With brand accounts in hand, we can self-sample to fashion creators in North America, Europe, and the Middle East — building a network of micro- and mid-tier influencers who organically amplify Fuggler apparel.

擁有品牌賬號後，可自主向北美、歐洲及中東的時尚達人寄樣，構建能有機放大Fuggler服飾聲量的紅人網絡。

The Bio link on Instagram and TikTok Shop tag function complete the full conversion funnel: Discover → Desire → Purchase. Every post is a potential storefront door.

Bio鏈接與TikTok商品標籤形成完整轉化閉環：發現 → 渴望 → 購買。每篇貼文即是一扇潛在的店面入口。

就社媒測款模式而言：若每輪週期中有2–3款經驗證的OEM設計獲市場認可，此模式已可自我覆蓋成本——因為設計在生產投入前已由市場需求篩選，從根本上降低庫存風險。

07 · Brand Governance & Safeguards

Respecting the IP, Protecting the Brand 尊重IP、保護品牌

We understand that Libertas has a responsibility to protect the Fuggler IP, and that our proposal changes the current governance model. We therefore propose the following safeguards:

我們理解Libertas有責任保護Fuggler IP，本提案亦改變了現行治理結構。為此，我們提出以下保障措施：

Concern · 關注點 Our Proposed Safeguard · 建議保障措施
Brand consistency / 品牌一致性 We will submit a visual brand kit (fonts, colours, logo usage, tone of voice guide) to Libertas for approval before any account goes live.
Content quality / 內容質量 A content calendar shared with Libertas monthly for visibility (not approval); major campaign posts shared 5 business days in advance.
IP scope / IP範圍 Accounts are strictly fashion/apparel-focused. We will not post about plush products or compete with ZURU's existing content territory.
KOL selection / 達人篩選 We will maintain a KOL briefing document defining categories of influencers we will and will not work with, shared with Libertas on request.
Account ownership / 賬號歸屬 Fir Ventures will maintain the accounts; Libertas will be listed as a secondary admin for oversight purposes.
08 · Conclusion

A Simple Idea, Backed by Evidence 一個簡單的想法，有據可依

Fuggler is a beloved IP with strong plush traction and an untapped opportunity in fashion. The apparel category's underperformance on Amazon is not a product problem — it is a visibility problem. Social media is where fashion consumers discover new brands, and right now Fuggler has no presence in that space for apparel.

Fuggler是一個深受喜愛的IP，毛絨品類表現強勁，服飾品類則尚待開發。服飾在Amazon的表現欠佳並非產品問題，而是曝光問題。社交媒體是時尚消費者發現新品牌的主要渠道，而Fuggler服飾目前在這一空間中幾乎缺席。

POP MART's journey — from a single social account to a 15+ country localised matrix generating billions in overseas revenue — demonstrates that social infrastructure is not a vanity exercise. It is the foundation of international consumer brand building. We are not asking to replicate POP MART. We are asking for the same starting point they once had: a brand account, a vertical focus, and the autonomy to move at market speed.

泡泡瑪特的歷程——從單一社媒賬號到覆蓋15個國家以上的本地化矩陣，並帶動數十億海外收入——證明社媒基礎設施並非虛榮工程，而是國際消費品牌建設的根基。我們無意複製泡泡瑪特的規模，只是希望擁有與他們當初相同的起點：一個品牌賬號、一個垂類焦點，以及以市場速度運作的自主性。
